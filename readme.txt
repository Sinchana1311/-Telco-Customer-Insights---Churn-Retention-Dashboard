Project Overview
 This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer churn across services, contracts, tenure, and billing behavior, transforming raw telecom data into actionable business insights.

Key Dashboard Views & Insights

 The Home page is structured around key business questions such as why customers churn, who they are, what services they use, and what account types they have. Interactive GIF-based buttons guide users to detailed pages on churn trends, customer insights, service analysis, and account analysis, enabling smooth and intuitive navigation across the dashboard.

Churn Overview
 Get a high-level snapshot of churn health with KPIs such as total customers, churn rate, active customers, and revenue at risk. Identify high-risk segments and overall churn trends at a glance.

Customer Profile Analysis
 Analyze churn behavior across demographics such as gender, senior citizenship, tenure groups, and support usage. Understand who is churning and why through behavioral and demographic patterns.

Services & Contract Analysis
 Dive deep into how services like internet type, phone service, and streaming options influence churn. Compare service-level churn against benchmarks and identify combinations driving customer attrition.

Billing & Payment Analysis
 Evaluate how billing methods, payment types, and paperless billing preferences impact churn and retention. Highlight risky payment behaviors and revenue leakage areas.

Interactive Navigation & Storytelling
 Designed with guided navigation, advanced visuals, decomposition trees, and benchmarking to help stakeholders explore insights intuitively and take informed actions.
